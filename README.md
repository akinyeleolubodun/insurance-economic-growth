### Objective

#### Research Questions
- What is the impact of Life insurance businesses penetration on economic growth in Nigeria?
- What is the impact of Non-Life insurance businesses penetration on economic growth in Nigeria?
- What is the impact of Life insurance businesses density on economic growth in Nigeria?
- What is the impact of Non-Life insurance businesses density on economic growth in Nigeria?

#### Research Hypotheses
- H0 - Life insurance businesses penetration has no significant impact on economic growth in Nigeria.
- H0 - Non-Life insurance businesses penetration has a significant impact on economic growth in Nigeria.
- H0 - Life insurance businesses density has no significant impact on economic growth in Nigeria.
- H0 - Non-Life insurance businesses density has a significant impact on economic growth in Nigeria.

The primary hypothesis seeks to ascertain the collective impact of all independent variables, GDP and Urban Population, which represent economic growth, on the dependent variables, Gross Premium income, reflecting the performance of the insurance sector.

Where, Insurance density represents the average per capita premiums collected, and insurance penetration represent the contribution of the insurance sector to the GDP.
